Campus Life

Even though it is in the farthest corner of Eskişehir , studying on 2 Eylül campus is very useful and fun for us students. Even though we have to race each other to get on the bus from time to time, we think it's fun. A lot of beautiful images are waiting for you, as our friend above videotaped. There are many social environments, cafes and lawns :). By the way, let me tell you that if you are a very good student, maybe you can see a military jet from the jet base next to you.
